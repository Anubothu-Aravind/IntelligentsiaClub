Goal of Intelligentsia Club

Intelligentsia fosters collaboration for students to explore AI and ML through hands-on projects, workshops, and discussions, bridging theory and real-world applications.

Are any skills needed to join Intelligentsia?

No prior experience is required. We welcome all students interested in AI/ML, offering support for both beginners and advanced learners.

What AI/ML projects does Intelligentsia focus on?

We work on basic models, NLP, computer vision, and reinforcement learning. Members can propose their own projects.

How often are meetings and workshops held?

Weekly meetings and bi-monthly workshops cover topics like neural networks and AI tools (TensorFlow, PyTorch).

Does Intelligentsia collaborate with other clubs or organizations?

Yes, we partner with other technical clubs and external organizations for joint projects, hackathons, and interdisciplinary events.

Is there a system for feedback and suggestions?

Yes, members can give feedback during meetings or submit ideas through an online form or directly to the leadership team.
